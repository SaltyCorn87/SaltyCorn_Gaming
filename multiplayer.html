What is multiplayer gaming?

Multiplayer gaming is any game experience where more than one player participates, either together in the same room (local) or connected through a network (LAN/online).

Early multiplayer

  • Local multiplayer: split-screen and shared-device play
  • Arcade competition: high scores and head-to-head cabinets

Early Era

Consoles began with local multiplayer using split-screen and shared controllers.

Online Services

Broadband Internet enabled consoles to connect to online services, allowing players to compete globally instead of only locally.

Modern Consoles

Today’s consoles support voice chat, matchmaking, downloadable updates, and cross-platform play.
